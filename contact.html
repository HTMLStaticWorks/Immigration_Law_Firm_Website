Common Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your consultation with our legal experts.

Is the first consultation free?

Yes, we provide a 15-minute initial assessment to understand your case and determine the basic eligibility pathways.

What documents should I prepare?

Please have your current passport, any previous visa rejection letters, and recent educational/employment certificates ready.

How long does the process take?

Processing times vary by country and visa type, but typically range from 3 to 18 months depending on local authority efficiency.

Can you help with emergency appeals?

Absolutely. Our emergency litigation team can file immediate stay-of-removal requests or appeal urgent visa rejections.
