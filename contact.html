Common Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your consultation with our legal experts.

Is the first consultation free?
Yes, we provide a 15-minute initial assessment to understand your case and determine the basic eligibility pathways. There is no obligation to continue.
What documents should I prepare?
Please have your current passport, any previous visa rejection letters, and recent educational or employment certificates ready. Digal copies are preferred for faster review.
How long does the process take?
Processing times vary significantly by country and visa type. Typically, it ranges from 3 to 18 months. We provide real-time tracking through our client portal to keep you updated.
Can you help with emergency appeals?
Absolutely. Our emergency litigation team can file immediate stay-of-removal requests or appeal urgent visa rejections within 24 to 48 hours of instruction.
